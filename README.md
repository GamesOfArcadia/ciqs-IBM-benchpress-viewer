## Abstract

We propose a reformulation of general relativity in which time is not a fundamental parameter. 
The framework, causal iteration, takes physical state and state transition as its primitive constituents, 
replacing the temporal metric component with a parameter state coordinate phi representing accumulated local physical change. 
The metric signature becomes (+,+,+,+). 

We show that this structural change removes the precondition for the Penrose-Hawking singularity theorems, 
which require closed trapped surfaces defined by the Lorentzian signature - surfaces that cannot exist in a Riemannian manifold. 
Singularities are therefore absent by construction, not by modification of the field equations. 

We further show that Lorentzian causal structure: light cones, invariant intervals, and causal ordering, 
is not lost but emerges from the phi-iteration transformation structure: the measurable invariant between inertial frames takes Lorentzian form as a direct consequence of the universal iteration rate. 
The framework reduces to standard general relativity in all confirmed observational regimes. 
The constructive derivation, including the field equations, geodesics, black hole interior geometry, and cosmological predictions, is developed in the companion paper.
